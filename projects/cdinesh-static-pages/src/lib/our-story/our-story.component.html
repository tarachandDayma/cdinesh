Beyond csr

Our founder Mr. Dinesh Shah at a young age started working at the Kinara Shop his father owned in his ancestral village Thalvada.
During this phase of his life he faced a lot of hardships for basic necessities such as access to healthcare and education. It was his vision in life to always give back to the community and the people who helped him in his journey to success.

He is the Founding Father of Smt. H A Shah General Hospital in Satlasana, that he named after his mother. The Hospital was established in 1992. The hospital treats on average 40,000 people every year. It has become the primary source for healthcare for the surrounding 20 villages. Till date it has provided Cataract surgery to 30,000 people for free of cost.

AM Shah High School in Thalvada, is another initiative by Mr. Dinesh Shah founded in 1999. The High School provides free education for all students between 9th to 12th grade. A Devotee of Jainism, we have also established Dharamshalas in Ayodhyapuram, Nageshwar and Upashres in Thalvada and Ayodhyapuram.
